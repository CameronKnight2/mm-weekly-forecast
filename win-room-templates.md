# IES Win Room — Deal Support Templates

---

## Version 1: Quick Post (Slack-Native)

Copy/paste directly into the Win Room channel for fast visibility.

```
🏢 *Account:* [Company Name]
💰 *Deal Size:* $[ARR/TCV]
📅 *Close Date:* [Target Date]
👤 *AE/Sales Leader:* [Name]

*Ask Type:* [Pricing | Term | Product Confidence/Roadmap | Services | Exec Support/Relationship]

*The Ask:*
> [1-2 sentences — what specifically do you need and from whom?]

*Why It Matters:*
> [1-2 sentences — what's at stake, what does winning this unlock?]

*Blocker / Risk if No Action:*
> [What happens if we don't act?]
```

## Version 2: Detailed Deal Brief

Use when the deal needs fuller context — paste into a Slack canvas, thread, or attach as a doc.

```
═══════════════════════════════════════
        IES WIN ROOM — DEAL BRIEF
═══════════════════════════════════════

DEAL OVERVIEW
─────────────────────────────────────
Account:            [Company Name]
Industry/Vertical:  [e.g., Construction, Professional Services]
Deal Size (ARR):    $[Amount]
Contract Term:      [e.g., 12 mo / 24 mo / 36 mo]
Target Close Date:  [Date]
Deal Stage:         [e.g., Negotiation, Procurement, Legal]
AE / Sales Leader:  [Name]
SE / Overlay:       [Name, if applicable]

CUSTOMER CONTEXT
─────────────────────────────────────
Current Stack:      [What are they using today? QBO, competitor, manual?]
Employee Count:     [Approx. size]
Decision Makers:    [Key contacts & titles involved in the decision]
Champion:           [Internal advocate, if identified]

WHAT WE'RE ASKING FOR
─────────────────────────────────────
Primary Ask Type (check all that apply):
  [ ] Pricing — Exception or discount approval needed
  [ ] Term — Non-standard contract term or structure
  [ ] Product Confidence / Roadmap — Customer needs assurance on capability or direction
  [ ] Services — Implementation, onboarding, or professional services support
  [ ] Exec Support / Relationship — Exec-to-exec engagement or direct outreach

Specific Ask:
[Detail exactly what you need. Examples:
 - "Need VP/SVP-level call with their CFO to reinforce platform direction"
 - "Requesting 20% pricing exception on Tier 3 — competitive pressure from NetSuite"
 - "Customer wants roadmap commitment on [feature] before signing"
 - "Need expedited booking process — customer fiscal year ends 3/31"]

Ideal Timeline:
[By when does this action need to happen to impact the deal?]

DEAL DYNAMICS
─────────────────────────────────────
Why We Win:
[What's working in our favor? Relationship, product fit, price, etc.]

Why We Lose:
[What's the biggest risk? Competitor, budget, internal politics, missing feature?]

Competitive Landscape:
[Who else is in the deal? What's their positioning?]

IMPACT
─────────────────────────────────────
Revenue at Stake:       $[ARR]
Expansion Potential:    [Is there a larger land-and-expand play?]
Strategic Value:        [Logo value, vertical reference, competitive displacement, etc.]

STATUS / NEXT STEPS
─────────────────────────────────────
Current Status:
[Where does the deal stand right now?]

Next Milestone:
[What's the next concrete step if we get the support requested?]

═══════════════════════════════════════
```

### Usage Guidance for Sales Leaders

| Situation | Use |
|---|---|
| Need a fast exec nudge or simple approval | **Version 1 — Quick Post** directly in channel |
| Complex deal, multiple levers, or unfamiliar account | **Version 2 — Deal Brief** as a thread or canvas |
| Follow-up / status update on a prior ask | Reply in the original thread with updated status |

**Tips for getting fast action:**
- Lead with the ask — don't bury it
- Be specific about *who* you need and *by when*
- Tag the relevant exec or leader if you know who can help
- Update the thread when the situation changes so the room stays current
